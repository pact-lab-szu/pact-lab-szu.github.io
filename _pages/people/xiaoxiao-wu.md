## About Me
Xiaoxiao Wu is an Associate Professor at Shenzhen University and a faculty member of PLAN Lab. Her research combines social-network mining, distributed intelligence, and AI-driven applications.

## Research Interests
- Social network data mining
- Community and hotspot discovery
- Distributed algorithms, signal processing, and AI applications

## Recruitment
The group welcomes postdoctoral researchers and graduate students, especially those interested in social networks and artificial intelligence.
