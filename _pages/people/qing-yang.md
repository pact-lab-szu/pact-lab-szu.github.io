## About Me
Qing Yang is an Associate Professor at Shenzhen University and a faculty member of PLAN Lab. Her research focuses on blockchain infrastructure and decentralized systems.

## Research Interests
- High-performance consensus algorithms
- Smart contracts and blockchain applications
- Decentralized distributed energy systems
