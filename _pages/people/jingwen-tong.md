## About Me
Jingwen Tong is an Assistant Professor at Shenzhen University and a faculty member of PLAN Lab. His research focuses on LLM-based AI agents, multi-armed bandits, and next-generation wireless networks.

## Research Interests
- LLM-based AI agents for wireless systems
- Multi-armed bandit learning and sequential decision-making
- Wireless network optimization and resource management

## Recruitment
The group recruits students interested in AI and wireless communications, especially at the intersection of LLM agents and network intelligence.
