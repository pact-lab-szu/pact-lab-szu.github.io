## About Me
Shengli Zhang is a Professor at Shenzhen University and a core faculty member of PLAN Lab. His work covers intelligent agent applications, agent-network systems, and digital-asset/Web3 technologies.

## Research Interests
- LLM-based agent applications
- Agent network architecture
- Digital assets and Web3

## Recruitment
Students interested in AI agents and networked intelligence are welcome to contact Prof. Zhang.
